## Common Success Patterns:

| Pattern | Example |
|---------|---------|
| Strong demo video | v0, Bolt |
| Pre-built audience | AI SaaS Launcher |
| Developer focus | Most top AI products |
| Free tier | Attracts votes |
| Real-time engagement | Reply to every comment |

## Metrics to Aim For:
- 100+ upvotes in first 4 hours
- 20+ comments
- #1-3 product of the day
- 1000+ email signups from PH

## What Works:
1. **Video demo** (2 min max)
2. **Clear tagline**
3. **Early access/freetier**
4. **Community engagement**
5. **Maker story**

## Lessons:
- First 4 hours matter most
- Reply to every comment personally
- Don't launch alone - build audience first
- Share behind-the-scenes story
