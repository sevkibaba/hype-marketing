# Product Hunt Launch for AI/Edge Products

## Quick Research Summary

### Key Findings:
1. **AI products can reach #1-2** on PH with right strategy
2. **Treat it like a campaign**, not a post
3. **Pre-launch audience building** is critical
4. **First 2-4 hours** determine ranking

### Top Strategies:

#### Pre-Launch (2-4 weeks before)
- Build email list of PH hunters
- Engage in PH community (comments, discussions)
- Get maker interviews scheduled
- Prepare launch assets (video, images, copy)
- Tease on social media

#### Launch Day
- Post at **6 AM PT** (12 PM Istanbul)
- First 4 hours = critical for ranking
- Get 50+ upvotes in first hour
- Reply to EVERY comment
- Update based on feedback

#### Post-Launch
- Thank all supporters
- Collect testimonials
- Pitch to journalists
- Continue engagement

### AI Product Specifics:
- Demo video is crucial
- Clear use case explanation
- Show, don't just tell
- Pricing transparency helps
- Free tier attracts more votes

### Useful Tools:
- PHLauncher - AI-powered campaign help
- Product Hunt Scheduler
- Early access email lists

## Case Studies:
- AI SaaS Launcher: #2 product of the day
- Many AI tools reached #1 with community-led launches

## Notes from Research:
- "A Product Hunt launch can still be one of the fastest ways for a bootstrapped AI startup to get real distribution"
- Treat it like a campaign, not a post
