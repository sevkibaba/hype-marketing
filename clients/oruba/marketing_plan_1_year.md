## 📢 Marketing Kanalları

### Kanal 1: LinkedIn (Ağırlık: 30%)
- **Aktiviteler:**
  - Daily: Engage with medical device groups
  - Weekly: Post case studies, testimonials
  - Monthly: LinkedIn Ads targeting "medical device distributor", "hospital procurement"
- **Bütçe:** $500/ay
- **Araçlar:** LinkedIn Sales Navigator, HeyReach

### Kanal 2: Email Outreach (Ağırlık: 25%)
- **Aktiviteler:**
  - Research: Medical device distributors in target countries
  - Weekly: 50-100 personalized emails
  - Follow-up sequences (5 email serisi)
- **Bütçe:** $200/ay (Apollo, ZoomInfo data)
- **Araçlar:** Apollo, Mailchimp

### Kanal 3: Medical Trade Shows (Ağırlık: 20%)
- **Etkinlikler:**
  - Medica (Almanya) - Kasım
  - Arab Health (UAE) - Ocak
  - EAU (Avrupa Üroloji) - Mart
  - FIME (ABD) - Ağustos
- **Bütçe:** $1000/ay (stand, travel)

### Kanal 4: Content Marketing (Ağırlık: 15%)
- **İçerikler:**
  - Blog: Haftada 2 post
  - Whitepaper: "Future of Uroflowmetry"
  - Case Studies: Mevcut hastanelerden
  - Video: Testimonial, demo, how-it-works
- **Bütçe:** $300/ay
- **Araçlar:** WordPress, Canva, Loom

### Kanal 5: SEO & Google Ads (Ağırlık: 10%)
- **Aktiviteler:**
  - Keyword research: "uroflowmetry machine", "automatic urine flowmeter"
  - On-page SEO
  - Google Ads: Medical keywords
- **Bütçe:** $500/ay
