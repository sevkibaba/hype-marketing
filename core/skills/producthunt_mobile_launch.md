# Product Hunt Mobile App Launch

Skill for launching mobile apps on Product Hunt.

## Overview

Product Hunt is one of the fastest free distribution channels for mobile apps. This skill helps plan and execute a successful PH launch.

## When to Use

Use this skill when:
- Client has a new mobile app (iOS/Android)
- App is ready to launch or in pre-launch phase
- Looking for free user acquisition channel
- Need to build initial traction

## Key Facts

| Fact | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **Best for** | New mobile apps (iOS/Android) |
| **Realistic goal** | Top 10 (200-400 upvotes) |
| **Best case** | #1 Product of the Day (800+ upvotes) |
| **Launch time** | 12:01 AM Pacific |
| **Minimum prep** | 30 days |
| **Minimum supporters** | 200-300 |

## The Process

### 1. Pre-Launch (30 Days Before)

| Week | Actions |
|------|---------|
| Week 4 | Create PH teaser page; start community engagement |
| Week 3 | Build supporter list (200+ people) |
| Week 2 | Finalize assets (screenshots, video, copy) |
| Week 1 | Prepare maker comment; test all links |

### 2. Hunter Strategy

**Option A: Self-Hunt (Recommended)**
- You know your app best
- Full control over messaging
- Most mobile app makers do this

**Option B: Professional Hunter**
- Find hunters with mobile app experience
- Contact 2-3 weeks before launch
- Be short and professional

### 3. Launch Day Timeline

| Time (PT) | Activity |
|-----------|----------|
| 12:01 AM | Launch goes live |
| 12-6 AM | Personal outreach to supporters |
| 6-9 AM | Social media announcements |
| 9 AM-12 PM | Peak engagement period |
| 12-6 PM | Email list (staggered) |
| 6-9 PM | Final push |
| 9 PM-12 AM | Close out and thank everyone |

### 4. Mobile App-Specific Requirements

- **Device mockups**: Show app on actual phones
- **GIFs**: Show user flows in gallery
- **Short URL**: Bit.ly for App Store links
- **QR code**: In gallery for easy access
- **Video**: Screen recording under 2 minutes

### 5. Topics to Use

Include these:
- Mobile App
- iOS (if applicable)
- Android (if applicable)
- Your app's category
- AI (if your app uses AI)

## Success Metrics

| Outcome | Upvotes | Placement |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| Poor | 50-100 | Not ranked |
| Average | 200-400 | Top 10 |
| Good | 500-800 | Top 5 |
| Great | 800+ | #1 Product of the Day |

## Quick Wins

1. **Check PH account** - Create today if needed
2. **Start teaser page** - At least 3 weeks before
3. **Build supporter list** - 200+ people
4. **Prepare device mockups** - High quality
5. **Shorten App Store URL** - Make it easy to download
